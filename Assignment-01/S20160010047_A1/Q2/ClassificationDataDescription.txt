The dataset is the collection of the 911 still images of seven common human actions. These classes are: Interacting with computer, Photographing, Playing Instrument, Riding Bike, Riding Horse, Running, Walking. Use 75% data for training and 25% for testing.

Class labels:

1 - Interacting with computer
2 - Riding Horse
3 - Riding Bike
4 - Playing Instrument
5 - Photographing
6 - Running
7 - Walking
